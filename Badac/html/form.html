Subject: Drug Test Consent Waiver

           I, the undersigned, hereby consent to undergo a drug test as part of the investigation concerning suspected drug use related to [specific individual or situation] in Barangay Sta. Lucia.

I acknowledge the following:

1. Purpose of Testing: The drug test is conducted to ensure community safety and compliance with barangay policies.
2. Confidentiality: I understand that the results of the drug test will be kept confidential and shared only with authorized personnel involved in the investigation.
3. Voluntary Participation: My participation in this drug testing process is voluntary; however, I recognize that refusal may impact the investigation.
4. Testing Procedures: I consent to the collection and testing of my specimen (urine, saliva, etc.) by authorized personnel, in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.
5. Release of Liability: I release the Barangay Drug Abuse Council and its representatives from any liability resulting from the testing process or the disclosure of test results.
6. Right to Review Results: I have the right to review the results of the test and, if necessary, contest any findings I believe to be incorrect.
By signing below, I acknowledge that I have read and understood this consent waiver and agree to participate in the drug testing program.
